There are so many ways you can get involved with Heart Helpers, whether you're taking part in a National Event or a local fundraiser, anything you can do helps!

Our National Events take place four times a year, one per season. But plenty of local branches host their own activities all year round. To check out what your local branch is doing, visit our Contact Us page for their details. We'll then pass on your query to your local branch who will be in touch with you.

Registration for our next National Event: Summer Solstice Event 2023 is now open. Use the form below to register, and await your welcome pack via email which will give you all the information you need. If you still have any questions, use the form on Contact Us!
