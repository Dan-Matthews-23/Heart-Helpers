Mary's story

"I was born with a heart defect which means my heartbeat is a little different to everyone else's. Sometimes my heart just isn't enough on its own to get blood around my body. For that, I need a little extra help. I have a small device - called an ICD - attached to my heart. It sends an electric shock to my heart if it starts beating irregularly, and without this device, I could die."
