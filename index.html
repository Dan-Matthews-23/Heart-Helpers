Read Mary's story

"I was born with a heart defect which means my heartbeat is a little different to everyone else's. Sometimes my heart just isn't enough on its own to get blood around my body. For that, I need a little extra help. I have a small device - called an ICD - attached to my heart. It sends an electric shock to my heart if it starts beating irregularly, and without this device, I could die. I need to have regular appointments with my consultant to make sure it's still working properly. I need to be careful not to move too much in case the leads displace, and even then there's always the chance that the ICD might stop working as it should, as everything else electronic. With your help, we can fund vital research into the causes, treatment, and eventually, cure of all heart disease. It really is so important we do everything we can."
